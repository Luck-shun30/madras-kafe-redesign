Philosophy

Fresh and Organic


At Madras Kafe, we believe that it is Health that comes first. With our use of local fresh vegetables and heart healthy olive oil in all our cooking and inclusion of highly nutritious ingredients, we have attempted to do just that.



Fast Casual


As a Fast Casual service to the greater Chicago area residents, Madras Kafe serves high quality "made-to-order" food as well as its combo platters, to accommodate changing customer food interests.



Gourmet Indian


Madras Kafe's menu covers all popular cuisines from the four corners of India, in their most authentic and their Gourmet forms.
